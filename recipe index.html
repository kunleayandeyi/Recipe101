Peppered Puffpuff Recipe




Ingreients

  • Flour 2 cups
  • 2 tea spoon of yeast
  • 1/4 cup of sugar
  • 1 tea spoon of grinded pepper (ata gungun)
  • 1/2 tea spoon powder nutmeg
  • chopped red onion
  • chopped red pepper(not scotch bonnet)
  • 1/2 cup of water or more

Preparation Steps

step 1: sieve the flour into a bowl and add the quantified yeast, sugar, nut meg and grinded pepper and mix evenly

step 2: mix the chopped red pepper and chopped red onion to the bowl content

step 3: add water and mix with hand till you have a thick paste mixture. The thickness determines the thickness of your puffpuff. I like my puffpuff thick, how do you like yours?

step 4: cover the bowl and place your the bowl in a warm environment for minimum of 1 hour to allow the yeast to ferment

step 5: put a deep pot on fire add enough oil to allow for deep frying and allow the oil to get hot, then reduce the heat of your cooker to allow even frying. Start scooping the mixture with your palm and drop it in a ball-like shape into the hot oil, allow to fry and turn it until u a colour has seen in the image above. Then pack it into your plate

you can garnish and enjoy your puffpuff with cold juice or drink

Tips

Tip 1: Ensure the correct quantity of yeast and please dont be tempted to add baking powder as this is not required for puffpuff

Tip 2: To avoid your Puffpuff from getting burnt without getting done inside ensure enough oil and ensure controlled heat. u can reduce the heat to mid-level

Tip 3: You can place a paper towel in your plate when packing your done puffpuff into plate
